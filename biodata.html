VISSION AND MISSION

Vission

Raising a righteous man guided by the Quran and Sunnah who succeeds in this world and the hereafter.

Mission

  • To care for, nurture, and provide the best services to the residents of the Darul Mizan Orphanage and Welfare Home for Orphans and the Needy.

  • Being a mediator and facilitator for those in need of advocacy.

  • To become a transparent and efficient welfare center in order to provide a conducive environment for orphans and the underprivileged.
