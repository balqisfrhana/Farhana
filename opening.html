OPENING REMARKS

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. I extend a warm welcome and heartfelt gratitude to all visitors who have chosen the Orphanage & Underprivileged Welfare Home as your destination. Truly, "So race to [all that is] good."

The main objective of the Kelantan Human Development Welfare Organization in establishing the orphanage for orphans and the underprivileged is to provide them with spiritual and physical education, so that they may become faithful servants with noble character. May your presence, sir/ma'am, bring blessings to all of us. We pray that Allah will reward you abundantly for the support and ongoing charitable deeds that you have done.
